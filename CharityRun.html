5K, 10K and Half Marathon Charity Run

Enter Volunteer Sponsor Event

Sunday 1st July 2018, Horsell Common, Woking.

Woking Lions are organising a Charity Run on Horsell Common, Woking on Sunday 1st July 2018. This will be an off road event running through beautiful woodland and open fields. All proceeds will go to good causes including Transform Housing, Woking & Sam Beare Hospices and Woking Lions Community Work.

Route

The route will run through horsell common and mclaren park. A map / gpx file will be posted here before the event.

Parking

Free Parking for up to 200 cars is provided at Fairoaks Airport. Please car share or walk to the race if you can. Further details regarding other car parks will be posted here.

Volunteer to help with Lions Charity Run 2018

This charity event is organised entirely by volunteers with all profits going to good causes. The 2018 run will build on the success of the previous two years with an increased numbers of runners and a bigger event village. We need your support to make this even more successful than the previous two years. If you would like to help, please sign up and you will be accepted as a volunteer. We will contact you nearer the time and try and match you to a role with your preferences.
