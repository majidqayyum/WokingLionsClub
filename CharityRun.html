Charity Cross Country Run

Enter Sponsor Event

Sunday 18th June 2017, Horsell Common, Woking.

Woking Lions are organising a Charity Cross Country Run on Horsell Common, Woking on Sunday 18th June 2017. This will be an off road event running through beautiful woodland and open fields. All proceeds will go to good causes including York Road Project and Woking Lions Community Work.


Where is it?

Fairoaks Airport
Chertsey Road
Chobham
Woking
GU24 8HU

When is it?

Sunday 18th June 2017


Registration Desk opens at 8 am (TBC)

10K and Half Marathon Races starts at 10:00 am (TBC)

5K Race starts at 10:15 am (TBC).

Please check the times near the event since they may change slightly.

Route

The route will run through horsell common and mclaren park. A map / gpx file will be posted here before the event.

Parking

Parking for up to 150 cars is provided at Fairoaks Airport. Please car share or walk to the race if you can.

How much does it cost?

Online registration, enter individually or as a Team.

Half Marathon Run is £29 (£25 before 31st Jan 2017) + booking fee

10K Run is £19 (£16 before 31st Jan 2017) + booking fee

5K Run is £14 (£12 before 31st Jan 2017) + booking fee

Spectators are free.

Places limited to 250 runners.

There will be Prizes in each category and all finishers will get a medal.

Facilities

Portable toilets, Refreshments, Water and First aid.
